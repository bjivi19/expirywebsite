What is Expiry?

Expiry is an innovative app designed to help you keep track of food expiration dates effortlessly. By scanning barcodes on food products, you can quickly add them to your virtual inventory. The app stores these expiration dates in an accessible database that is linked with your calendar and reminders apps.

With Expiry, you never have to worry about forgetting when food items are about to expire. The app sends you reminders in advance, helping you remember to consume or dispose of products before they go bad.
